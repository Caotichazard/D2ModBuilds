Destiny 2 BreadBuilds

This site was made with the purpose of making it easier to theorycraft and make builds, and also facilitate sharing these builds
To make this as intuitive as possible I worked hard to immitate the UI ingame

To whom it may interes, some developer notes at the bottom

To export a build, just click on the export button, it will create a long line of letters, just copy that and send to anyone you want to share the build with. If you have that line of letters, just drop that on the text box bellow and click import build and it will fill out everything (the build string as I call it is really long, specially when you fill it with mods)
